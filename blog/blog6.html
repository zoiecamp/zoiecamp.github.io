Back

During these 2 weeks, I spent the majority of my time working on the shader effect for the building ranges. I was finally able to get it finished, which was a massive sense of relief and accomplishment, as I’ve spent many many weeks thinking that it just wasn’t ever going to happen, but I’m really proud of the final result. After that, I was able to move on to a few other tasks - implementing a quick credits screen on the home page and starting on a charm system for adventurers.

(1h 30m) Implementing Charm System

Finally, I was tasked with implementing a quick charm system. In the game, charms are a type of gear that adventurers can equip and that give a specific type of benefit - some examples provided were resurrecting the user of the charm when they die, or boosting the gold found in the dungeon. I started looking into this very quickly to figure out how to implement it, but I need to talk to other members at the meeting about the specifics of how the dungeon works before I can finish it up.
