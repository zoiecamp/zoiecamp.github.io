TinkerEngine

A simple multi-platform (Mac, Windows, and Linux) built in one semester.

Written in C++ with support for Lua scripting and a component architecture.

Uses SDL, GLM, LuaBridge, and Box2D.

Legend of Zelda Remaster

A recreation of the first dungeon of the original 1986 Legend of Zelda. Press 4 to switch to a custom level where you will solve puzzles and defeat enemies with the help of a trusty sidekick.

Teammate: Anna Stewart

My Contributions: Enemy movement and damage, Sword, Room transitions, Custom level mechanics

Created using Unity and C#

No Light Left

Rapid prototype built in two weeks.

Navigate through pitch-black levels in this high-stakes 2D platformer. Use your light carefully - its charge is limited, and its light attracts enemies.

Created using Unity and C#

Rainy Day Rush

Run, jump, and glide your way through this 2d side-scrolling platformer with nothing but your trusty umbrella as you try to make it to your interview. But be careful, you won't get that job easily; there are dangers lurking around every corner. Brave the weather and keep your suit clean to look your best for the interview - hope you get the job!

Teammates: Selena Wang, Arvin Bansal, Ryan Duan, Jason Zhou

My Contributions:

  • Designed and drew art for the level 1 and 2 backgrounds, text bubbles, buttons, and UI panels
  • Designed levels 1 and 2 alongside teammate Selena Wang
  • Implemented the checkpoint and dialogue systems
  • Implemented aesthetic details including parallax and scene transitions
  • Implemented the end scene at the top of the final skyscraper

Created using Unity and C#

Play on Itch.io
